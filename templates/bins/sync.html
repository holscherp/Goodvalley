How to find your Session ID
  1. Open pWarehouse8 in your browser and log in normally
  2. Go to Bins en Bodega and click any row to load data
  3. Press F12 to open DevTools → click the Network tab
  4. Find any request to HandleEvent in the list
  5. Click it → scroll to Request Headers → copy the _S_ID value

Sessions usually last a few hours. If sync fails with "session expired", repeat the steps above to get a fresh ID.
